What does HORUS do?

Other companies claim to have "autonomous" capabilities but yet they still require sophisticated hardware and intrensic knowledge of their control systems to accomplish tasks. Horus takes things to the next level and automates everything from power up to recharging in a simple, easy-to-use package.

With our ability to customize the payload in what Horus carries the possibilities are almost unlimited. From streaming video to dropping off supplies Horus can handle just about anything.

Free Updates

All future software updates will be made available for free to our customers and deployment is a snap to manage via our easy to use cloud based distribution tools. No more guessing what version each device is running!

Awesome features

Web based mission viewer

Control or watch in real-time as your fleet streams video / sensor information back from missions to the cloud.

Automated work tickets

Horus will schedule it's own maintenance tickets and even pull itself out of the flight rotation if needed.

Flight Planner

Easily create flight plans from scratch or clone an existing one from your repository using our browser based tools.

Wake on demand

Each Horus will check in with the cloud every 1-5 minutes to see if work has been assigned to it and autonomously carry out it's mission.

Cloud based analysis

Pipe your data stream through OpenCV or Spark for real-time or batch processing.

Historical Logs

Review telemetry and media captured from previous flights via our web based tools.
